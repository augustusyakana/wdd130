Overview

Purpose

Our goal here at Rafting, is to bring whitewater rafting to you in a fun, memorable and very unique way. It is our mission to provide exceptional service at a professional level in this industry. As strive we for greatness and to be recognized as the best at what we do, it would only be possible with you! Locally owned and themed with our culture and traditions, you will find that there is so much more to do here and learn about. Welcome to Rafting and Enjoy the ride!

Audience

Looking for something thrilling, scary or even nerve wrecking? Come to Rafting! You'll leave with more. We are looking for thrill-seakers and people who admire culture and traditions to come and see all the good things we have in store for you. When you enter, we will take you through a journey of mixed emotions. Scared, excited, thrilled, nervous, but all while learning about the unique culture, history, and traditions of our people! Come down to Rafting and we'll make sure you enjoy this ride!
