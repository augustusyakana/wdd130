Overview

Purpose

Establish an online presence and be able to showcase her artwork to her fans, followers and even visitors. Mimi'z Artz is an artist who specialize in creating art (drawing or paintings) to advocate for and amplify people's rights.

Purpose

Individuals who are passionate about social justice, equality and human rights. These individuals may consist of activists, advocates, and individuals who strive for positive societal change. These individuals appreciate art as a powerful medium to provoke thought, inspire empathy, and ingite conversations surrounding important social issues. This audience seeks artwork that resonates with their values, challenges the status quo, and fosters a sense of empowerment and unity among marginalized communities.

Introduction:

Mary is a young talented micronesian woman. She graduated from McKinley High School in Hawaii (Same high school The Rock graduated from). She dreams to one day inspire young artists to share their work and advocate for their beliefs through art. She is 20 years old and she started drawing at a very young age. She is inspired by social justice and people's rights. She believes that her artwork can bring awareness to this topic on a global level. This is only the beginning of the journey for her.
